Program

Note: A complete and publicly accessible program will be posted online by May 1st, 2026.

Session Format

The splinter session will consist of:

  • One/two invited review talks (20+4 minutes each)
  • Contributed talks (10+2 minutes each, 5-8 talks selected from submissions)
  • 2-minute Research Highlights/Poster Pop-ups (up to 5 presentations with a single slide)
  • Panel Discussion (20-25 minutes) - Summary of key insights and open exchange among all participants
